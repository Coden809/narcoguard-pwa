## üéØ What is NarcoGuard?

NarcoGuard is a comprehensive overdose prevention system featuring:

- **Automatic overdose detection** via continuous vitals monitoring
- **Instant naloxone deployment** when life-threatening patterns detected
- **Multi-source power** - Solar, kinetic, wireless, USB-C charging
- **Modular cartridge system** for easy naloxone replacement
- **24/7 health monitoring** - Heart rate, SpO2, temperature, respiratory rate

### ü§ñ Guardian AI Assistant
- Personal AI that monitors your safety around the clock
- Provides emergency guidance for CPR and naloxone administration
- Coordinates rescue responses with nearby heroes
- Offers recovery resources and wellness tracking
- Voice-activated hands-free emergency activation

### ü¶∏ Hero Network
- Community of trained responders carrying naloxone
- Real-time location sharing during emergencies
- AR-guided naloxone administration and CPR
- Protected by Good Samaritan laws
- Free training and certification programs

- Install on any device with one click
- Works offline for critical emergency functions
- Real-time vitals dashboard with Kalman filtering

- HIPAA-compliant privacy and security
